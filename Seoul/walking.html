2002년 FIFA 월드컵을 기념하여 조성된 공원이다.

한강변의 쓰레기 매립장이었던 난지도 쓰레기 매집장이 폐쇄된 자리와 그 주변에 조성하였으며,
평화의 공원, 하늘공원, 노을공원, 난지천공원, 난지한강공원 등 5개의 공원으로 이루어져 있다.

World Cup Park (English: World Cup Park) is located in Sangam-dong and Seongsan-dong, Mapo-gu, Seoul
It is a park built in commemoration of the 2002 FIFA World Cup.

Nanjido garbage landfill, which was a landfill on the Han River, was built around the closed site,
It consists of five parks: Peace Park, Sky Park, Noh Park, Nanji Stream Park, and Nanji Han River Park.

광화문 (Gwanghwamun)


광화문광장(光化門廣場)은 대한민국 서울특별시 종로구에 위치한 광장이다.

범위는 광화문에서 세종로 사거리 및 청계광장에 이르며, 폭 34m, 길이 740m의 상시 광장에,
평상시에는 차도이나 대규모 행사시에 광장으로 활용이 가능한 왕복 10차선 예비광장으로 구성되어 있다.

Gwanghwamun Square (Changhwamun Square) is a plaza located in Jongno-gu, Seoul, Korea.

The range reaches Sejongno intersection and Cheonggye Plaza from Gwanghwamun, and it is 34m wide and 740m long.
In normal times, it consists of a round-trip 10-lane preliminary plaza that can be used as a plaza in a driveway or large-scale event.

Cheonggyecheon () is a local river located inside Seoul, Korea, which belongs to the Han River water system and is a tributary of Jungnangcheon.
The longest origin is the Baekun-dong Valley located in Cheongun-dong, Jongno-gu,

It flows south and grows in size by combining Samcheongdong Stream in the underground near Cheonggye Plaza.

7017 in Seoul means that 'the highway built in 1970 is reborn as the way of 17 people in 2017'
It is the name of the urban regeneration park project of the named Seoul Station Highway.

The Seoul Metropolitan Government opened the Seoul Station High Street on May 20, 2017,
A structure connecting the nearby walkway and the high price will be installed and finally connected to a total of 17 walkways.

Seoul Forest is a park located at 685 Seongsu-dong 1-ga, Seongdong-gu, Seoul.

The Han River and Jungnangcheon stream flow near the park, the third largest park in Seoul after the Mapo-gu World Cup Park (1 million pyeong) and Songpa-gu Olympic Park (500,000 pyeong).

뚝섬한강공원 (Ttukseom Hangang Park)



뚝섬은 서울특별시 성동구 성수동 일대의 범람원으로,
지대가 낮아 한강에 홍수가 날 때마다 물길이 생겨 섬처럼 보였기에 섬이라고 하였다.

1980년대 초 한강종합개발사업에 의하여 한강을 직강화(直江化)하면서 남쪽의 많은 부분이 잘려 나갔고,
제방을 쌓으면서 완전한 육지화가 이루어졌다.

Ttukseom is a floodplain in Seongsu-dong, Seongdong-gu, Seoul,
It was called an island because it seemed like an island because of the low area and the waterways were created every time the Han River flooded.

In the early 1980s, the Han River was directly strengthened by the Han River comprehensive development project, and many parts of the south were cut off.
Full landfall was achieved as the embankment was built.

홍제천 (Hongjecheon)


홍제천(弘濟川)은 북한산에서 발원하여 서울특별시 종로구·서대문구·마포구의 일부

1974년 지방 2급 하천(현 지방하천)으로 지정되었고, 1983년과 1988년에 각각 하천 정비 기본계획을 수립해

Hongjecheon () originated from Bukhansan and is part of Jongno-gu, Seodaemun-gu, and Mapo-gu in Seoul
Or it is a local river that flows down the Han River, including the whole area.

It was designated as a second-class local river (now a local river) in 1974, and in 1983 and 1988, it established a basic plan for river maintenance, respectively.
As of February 1999, the number of rivers in the watershed, which amounted to 18.94 km, was completed.
